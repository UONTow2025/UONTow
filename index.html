The Age of Discovery
Over the first weeks of the campaign the colonial map will be mostly hidden, and therefore the first task is exploring the area.

Building a fleet is a costly endeavour, 10 gold to be exact. This opens up the door to partake in the colonial system and also builds a free port in one of their coastal tiles.

Each player with a fleet will be asked which direction they approach the map from and that area will be unveiled to them. They may choose to claim a tile visible to them. After game week 3 the whole map silhouette will be revealed.
