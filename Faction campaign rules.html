UON TOW campaign 2025 by Himmy

Faction campaign rules:

Dwarfs

Book of Grudges - When defeated by an opposing player or the general is destroyedin close combat, the next time the Dwarf player plays they may add a free Dragon Slayer or Doomseeker to their army. This bonus stacks up to three times but resets (i.e. no additional slayers) once the Dwarf player wins a game. The Dragon Slayer(s) or Doomseeker(s) may not purchase any magic items/runes and have +1 to leadership.

Bretonnia

Honor and Chivalry - Bretonnia players can start with 1 castle. Bretonnia armies can take 1 battle standard bearer for free.

Empire

Quantity is a Quality All Its Own - Empire armies start with one additional territory, and start with a castle.

Colomboâ€™s Legacy- An Empire player choosing this effect instead of the nuln bonus may start with an island and a small fort built on it

Nuln special rule - Nuln armies always start with an allowance of two units of war machine, regardless or not if they have a forrest. First forest does not add to the war machine limit.

High Elves

Masters of Magic - High Elf armies start with the ability to take a Level 2 wizard instead of a level 1 wizard.

Wood Elves

The Trees are Moving - One of the elven starting territories is always a Forest, additionally the war machine allowance from Forests can be used to recruit Treeman. Wood Elves armies start with the ability to take a Level 2 wizard instead of a level 1 wizard.

Warriors of Chaos

Raiders - May disable one of the defenders territory bonus of their choice during attacking.

Whispers of the Gods- May pick another opponent (except your battle opponent this round) to suffer from the lingering whispers of chaos. That player will randomly assign this effect to one unit(does not spread to attached characters or units a character a unit is attached to)- this unit suffers -1 leadership for the duration of the battle and must take a stupidity test on the first turn of the battle

Beasts of Chaos

It's a Giant! - Beastmen armies always start with an allowance of one unit in the large monsters and one unit of monstrous cavalry category, regardless or not if they have a Mountain. Each time a beast army wins, they may add 5% extra points to their army allowance, this bonus stacks until the army is defeated or if all infantry are destroyed (a draw does not count as a defeat, just a good scrap) and may stack up to three times. First 2 mountains does not add to the large monster/monstrous cav limit.

Tomb Kings

Mortuary Cult - Tomb King armies start with the ability to take a level 2 wizards if they are your general. Each time a Tomb King army wins, they may add 5% points to their army allowance, this bonus stacks up to three times and remains until the army's general is destroyed. Wins against other Vampire Counts or Tomb Kings players do not generate bonus stacks, although if the general is destroyed in those games it will still reset the bonus. Additionally, if the general is destroyed, next game there will be a -5% points penalty to their army allowance.

Orc and Goblins

WAAAGGHHH! - Each time an Orc and Goblin army wins, they may add 5% extra points to their army allowance, this bonus stacks until the army is defeated (a draw does not count as a defeat, just a good scrap) and may stack up to three times.

Dark Elves

Masters of Magic - Dark Elf armies start with the ability to take one Level 2 wizard instead of one level 1 wizard.

Chaos Dwarfs

Blackfire industry - Chaos Dwarfs armies start with the ability to take two war machines in their starting allocation instead of one.

Ogres

It's a Giant! - Ogre armies always start with an allowance of two units in the large monsters and two units in the monstrous cavalry category, regardless or not if they have a Mountain. First 2 mountains does not add to the large monster/monstrous cav limit.

Chaos Daemons

It's a Giant! - Chaos Daemon armies always start with an allowance of one unit in the large monsters or monstrous cavalry category, regardless or not if they havea Mountain. Gifts of Chaos - Chaos Daemon armies are unable to select common magic items - instead their Chaos Gifts and Daemonic Icons use their magic item allowance

Vampire Counts

Vampire Counts has ????????????? Growing Horde - Armies start with the ability to take a level 2 wizards if they are your general. Each time a Vampire Count army wins, they may add 5% points to their army allowance, this bonus stacks up to three times and remains until the army's general is destroyed. Wins against other Vampire Counts or Tomb Kings players do not generate bonus stacks, although if the general is destroyed in those games it will still reset the bonus. Additionally, if the general is destroyed, next game there will be a -5% points penalty to their army allowance.

Cathay

Far east- Cathay always start with an allowance of one units in the large monsters and one units in the war machine category, regardless or not if they have a Mountain. First mountains does not add to the large monster/monstrous cav limit. and first forest does not add to the war machine limit. Each turn Cathay can choose another faction to trade with, that player gets a 20% bonus to their gold income, and cathay gets 20% bonus to their gold income if they have lost 2 games in a row.
